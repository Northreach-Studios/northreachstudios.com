Game Project

Storms of Aether

Storms of Aether is an original fantasy strategy game built around meaningful progression, player agency, and a living world designed to reward thoughtful play over time.

Project Status

Early production with clear long-term direction.

The project is currently focused on defining its world, sharpening core systems, and building a foundation that can support steady development over time.

About the Game

A fantasy strategy experience with room to grow.

Storms of Aether is being shaped as a strategy game where decisions carry weight, progression feels earned, and the world itself supports long-term play rather than short-lived novelty.

The design direction prioritises original worldbuilding, clear strategic choices, and systems that remain readable while still offering depth. The goal is a game that feels deliberate in both structure and atmosphere.

From the start, the project is being approached as a living development journey. That means building a strong foundation first, then expanding with care as the game takes shape.

Development Philosophy

Clarity, fairness, and lasting quality.

Storms of Aether is being developed with an emphasis on fair systems, readable complexity, and strong internal consistency. Strategic depth should come from meaningful choices, not confusion.

The wider aim is to create a game that can evolve without losing its identity, with decisions guided by long-term quality rather than short-term noise.
