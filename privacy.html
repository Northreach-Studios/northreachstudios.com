Privacy Notice

Last updated: 30 May 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Northreach Studios, an independent game studio based in the United Kingdom. Northreach Studios is not currently registered as a UK limited company.

You can contact us about privacy matters at support@northreachstudios.com.

What Personal Data We Collect

This website does not currently use contact forms, accounts, analytics, advertising pixels, newsletter sign-ups, or payment processing.

If you email us, we will receive the personal data you choose to include, such as your name, email address, and message content.

The website is hosted by GitHub Pages. Hosting providers may process technical data needed to serve the site, such as IP address, browser information, device information, request time, and pages requested.

How We Use Personal Data

We use personal data only where needed to:

  • respond to messages and enquiries;
  • maintain the security and reliability of the website;
  • keep basic records of correspondence where necessary.

Lawful Basis

Where we respond to an enquiry, we rely on legitimate interests in communicating with people who contact the studio. If correspondence relates to a potential agreement or collaboration, processing may also be necessary to take steps before entering into a contract.

Sharing Data

We do not sell personal data. We may use service providers such as email and website hosting providers where needed to operate the studio and website.

How Long We Keep Data

We keep email correspondence only for as long as reasonably needed for the purpose it was sent, including follow-up, record keeping, or legal reasons.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to, or request a copy of your personal data. To make a request, email support@northreachstudios.com.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Changes To This Notice

We will update this notice if the website adds features such as analytics, newsletters, accounts, stores, or community tools.
